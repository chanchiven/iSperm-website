SQA-6100vet

Fully Automated Veterinary CASA System

Product Information

SQA-6100vet is a fully automated CASA (Computer-Assisted Sperm Analysis) system specifically designed for veterinary reproductive applications.
It enables precise and efficient evaluation of animal sperm parameters, including:

  • Motility and concentration
  • Kinetic parameters (VAP, VSL, VCL, etc.)
  • Morphology analysis
  • Immotile sperm ratio
  • Effective sperm count
  • And more.

Product Features

  • Fully automated, auto focus, auto tracing
  • 35°C adjustable heating stage
  • 3-step operation
  • Raw semen analysis
  • 30 seconds result, 30 frames
  • 6-chamber, fixed-depth, 20μm, counting chamber
  • Gaussian dynamic AI image algorithm
  • Support label printer

With its advanced optics, intelligent algorithms, and user-friendly workflow, this fully automated CASA system delivers exceptional accuracy and repeatability—making it an essential tool for breeding centers, veterinary clinics, and research labs focused on animal reproduction.
